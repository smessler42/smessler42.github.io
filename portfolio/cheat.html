Cheat, by Shayna Messler

She came every night. Her faucet shinier, her bowl cleaner. She was everything I could never become. I zip my bag; there wasn't much here. I lay back, letting the waves of time take me back to where all porcelain is from. He said he loved me, but he let that sink in.
